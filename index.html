Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Soonly Event Widget ("the App") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the application.

Information We Collect

The App itself does not collect personally identifiable information. Event data created inside the app is stored locally on your device.

Calendar Access

If you choose to import events from your device calendar, the App may request calendar permission. Calendar data is used only for importing events into the app and is not transmitted to external servers.

Advertising

The free version of the App may display advertisements using Google AdMob. AdMob may collect anonymous usage data according to Google's policies.

Learn more: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Premium Features

Premium purchases are processed securely through Google Play Billing. The App does not store payment information.

Data Storage

All event information is stored locally on your device unless future cloud synchronization features are enabled.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed toward children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated in the future. Changes will be reflected on this page.
